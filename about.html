About Company

Story about our hospital

We Offer a full range of Professional medical Health services to children, Adults, Couples, and Families.

Our Intensive Care Unit also known as the Critical Care Unit is a special department of the Hospital where we can provide around the clock continuous monitoring of our more seriously ill patients. The mission of Dominion specialist and diagnostics centre is to improve the health of the community and the world by setting the standard of excellence in medical education, research and clinical care.
